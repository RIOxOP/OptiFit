About OptiFit

OptiFit is an AI-powered health and fitness web application designed to help you achieve your wellness goals. Our platform provides personalized diet plans, workout routines, and Indian recipe suggestions based on your unique profile. You can also chat with our AI fitness coach for expert advice, tips, and motivation.

Key Features

  • Personalized Diet & Workout Plans: Get recommendations tailored to your age, gender, activity level, and preferences.
  • Recipe Generator: Discover healthy Indian recipes filtered by region, diet type, and allergens.
  • AI Chatbot: Ask fitness, nutrition, and wellness questions in English or Hindi.
  • BMI, BMR & TDEE Calculators: Scientific health metrics for accurate planning.
  • Modern UI: Responsive, mobile-friendly design with smooth animations.

Technologies Used

  • Python, Flask (backend web framework)
  • Pandas, scikit-learn (data processing & recipe search)
  • LangChain, Groq LLM (AI-powered recommendations & chatbot)
  • Tailwind CSS, Font Awesome (UI & icons)
  • HTML5, Jinja2 templates (frontend)
